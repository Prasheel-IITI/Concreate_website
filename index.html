Our Community

The Concreate club of IIT Indore was established in 2018 and since its formation it has been working for the betterment and upliftment of the engineering especially for the civil engineering department. The club will help you in exploring civil engineering through activities, lessons, and field trips.

We have successfully organised many events, workshops, seminars and have also started first Student Chapter in the college. Participating in club activities is a great way to enrich the undergraduate academic experience, and build connections.
